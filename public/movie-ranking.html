Michael's Top 3 Movies of All Time

From Action to Anime,
Grab Your Popcorn and Get Comfortable

The Dark Knight

Heath Ledger's portrayal of the Joker is one of the most memorable performances I have ever seen, and is the pinnacle of "tactful chaos."

Pokémon: The First Movie

A classic from my childhood, combining the fantastical elements of Pokémon with a surprisingly philosophical story about finding one's purpose in life.

Safe Haven

A favorite for both my wife and me, this is a wonderful story about how love prevails in the end, and what it takes to be a survivor.
